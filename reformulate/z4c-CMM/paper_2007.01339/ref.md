Bib keys from `characteristic_formulation.bib/.bbl`. Only calculation-relevant entries listed; purely motivational citations (detections, parameter estimation) omitted.

## Directly load-bearing

- **Bondi:1962px** — Bondi, van der Burg, Metzner (1962). Origin of the Bondi-Sachs metric form `eq:BondiSachsMetric` and falloffs `eq:BondiSachsFalloffs`.
- **Bishop:1997ik** — Bishop, Gomez, Lehner, Maharaj, Winicour (1997), "High-powered gravitational news". Original characteristic hypersurface equations (`eq:BondiHierarchy` content), geodesic scri+ equations `eq:GeodesicEquations`, original news formulas; the Section IV equations are a coordinate-transformed simplification of these.
- **Bishop:1998uk** — Bishop et al. (1998). Worldtube boundary-extraction procedure of Section II.B (null vector `eq:auto-11`, Bondi-like scalars `eq:BondiLikeScalarsFromBondiLikeMetric`).
- **Barkett:2019uae** — Barkett, Moxon, Scheel, Szilagyi (2019). Streamlined worldtube transformation without constant-r extrapolation; first-derivative identities eq:auto-14/eq:auto-15; logarithm-safe numerics for form-2 equations.
- **Handmer:2014qha** — Handmer & Szilagyi (2015). Spectral CCE in SpEC; matrix form eq:auto-26; source of the pure-gauge logarithm problem addressed in Section V.
- **Newman:1961qr** — Newman & Penrose (1962). Spin-weighted scalars, eth formalism, NP tetrad/spin-coefficient/Weyl-scalar framework of Section VI.
- **Babiuc:2008qy** — Babiuc, Bishop, Szilagyi, Winicour (2009). Tetrad motivating `eq:NP_tetrads`; alternative news formulas used for compatibility checks.
- **Boyle:2015nqa** — Boyle (2016). BMS transformations of Weyl scalars used for the PF→Bondi relations in `eq:bondi_weyl_scalars`.
- **companion_package** — Moxon, Mathematica companion notebook. Rederivation/verification of the hypersurface equations and spin-coefficient identities; primary machine-checkable evidence cited by the paper.
- **Barnich:2010eb** — Barnich & Troessaert. Bondi-Sachs gauge-fixing conditions equivalent to the step 1–5 transformation targets.
