# ref.md — references used directly / semi-directly in the calculations of arXiv:1010.0523v2

Direct calculational inputs:
- [Bernuzzi:2009ex] Bernuzzi & Hilditch, PRD 81 084003 (2010) — source of the Z4c formulation, conformal variables, spherical reduction, numerical code, and the ε_α = ε_χ = 0 characteristic variables. Inputs to: eq:Z4_ADM_1…eq:Conf_Constr_2, eq:auto-3…eq:auto-8, numerical Sec. V.
- [Kreiss70] Kreiss, Comm. Pure Appl. Math. 23, 277 (1970) — Kreiss theory: determinant condition, symmetrizer, well-posedness of strictly hyperbolic IBVPs. Inputs to: Appendix A (eq:def-system…eq:gen_estimate).
- [Ruiz:2007hg] Ruiz, Rinne & Sarbach, CQG 24, 6349 (2007) — notation for boundary geometry, frozen-coefficient transformation eq:backg-metricP, first order pseudo-differential reduction, high order BC iteration (eq:high-orderwave, eq:h-bc), toy-model estimates eq:phi-estimate, eq:full-estimate-W.
- [Kreiss:2006mi] Kreiss, Reula, Sarbach & Winicour — Laplace–Fourier treatment of wave-type IBVPs; reduction variables and estimates used in Sec. IV and Appendix B.
- [Hilditch:2010wp] Hilditch & Richter — analysis of formulations starting in fully second order form; basis for the 2+1 decomposition method of Sec. II.D.
- [Gundlach:2005eh] Gundlach et al. — Z4 constraint damping scheme (explicitly discarded here) and Z4–GHG equivalence.
- [Bona:2003fj, Bona:2003qn] Bona et al. — original Z4 formulation.
- [Bona:2004ky, Bona:2010wn] Bona et al. — L = 0 maximally dissipative constraint-compatible BCs that eq:general_CPBCs generalizes.
- [Gustafsson95, kreiss2001] Gustafsson, Kreiss & Oliger — Laplace–Fourier method, Parseval inversion, reduction to trivial initial data.
- [Agranovich:1972] Agranovich — extension of Kreiss theory to strongly hyperbolic systems of constant multiplicity.

Semi-direct (context, comparison, technique):
- [Nunez:2009wn] Núñez & Sarbach — CPBCs for BSSN (motivating comparison).
- [Buchman:2006xf, Buchman:2007pj] Buchman & Sarbach — absorbing BCs; inspiration for reflection coefficient eq:refcoef and high order conditions.
- [Rinne:2006vv, Rinne:2008vn, Rinne:2007ui] Rinne et al. — GHG boundary conditions, SpEC tests, reference-solution comparison methodology.
- [Calabrese:2005fp] Calabrese et al. — ghostzone-population implementation recipe.
- [Brugmann:2008zz] Brügmann et al. — BAM code and moving puncture gauge.
- [Bayliss:1980] Bayliss & Turkel — hierarchy of absorbing BCs for wave equations.
- [Hannam:2006vv] Hannam et al. — stationary trumpet slice of puncture evolutions.
- [Kreiss:2008ig] Kreiss et al. — energy method for second order wave systems with Sommerfeld-like BCs.
- [Taylor99b] Taylor — pseudo-differential operator theory (expected generalization).
- [Alcubierre08a] Alcubierre — textbook: hyperbolicity, algebraic constraint projection.
- [Hilditch:2010prep] Hilditch et al., in preparation — promised general-parameter analysis.
